Introduction

Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur and businessman who founded X.com in 1999 (which later became PayPal), SpaceX in 2002, and Tesla Motors in 2003. Musk became a multimillionaire in his late 20s when he sold his start-up company, Zip2, to a division of Compaq Computers.

Achievements

  • Co-founded PayPal
  • Founded SpaceX
  • Co-founded Tesla, Inc.
  • Founded Neuralink
  • Founded The Boring Company
  • Acquired Twitter (rebranded as X)

Companies

SpaceX

SpaceX is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transport services company. It was founded in 2002 with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

Tesla, Inc.

Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company. Tesla's current products include electric cars, battery energy storage, solar panels, and solar roof tiles.

Conclusion

Elon Musk is a dynamic entrepreneur whose vision and achievements continue to shape the future of technology and transportation. His relentless pursuit of innovation has made him a key figure in several industries.
